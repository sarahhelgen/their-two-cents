Designers Avatar of the designer Huerta Tipográfica Principal design Argentina Huerta Tipográfica is a collaborative Argentinian type foundry with a deep respect for design and typography. Founded in 2009, the company began as a place to meet, cooperate, and share experiences while collaborating on academic and commercial projects. Huerta Tipográfica develops custom and retail fonts with libre, proprietary, or exclusive licenses, and is strongly committed to creating innovative and functional type. Their award-winning work has been recognized by Letter.2, Tipos Latinos, and the Bienal Iberoamericana de Diseño.
